# Privacy Policy for Keep In Touch

**Last updated:** February 27, 2026

Keep In Touch is a privacy-first personal CRM app. Your relationship data stays on your device.

## Data Collection

- We do **not** collect any personal data (names, phone numbers, emails, or relationship details)
- We do **not** use advertising frameworks or sell data
- We do **not** require App Tracking Transparency (ATT) permission

## Anonymous Usage Analytics

- The app collects **anonymous usage statistics** via [TelemetryDeck](https://telemetrydeck.com), a privacy-first analytics service
- Data collected includes: which features are used (e.g., "a connection was logged"), app open/close events, and onboarding completion — **never** the content of your data
- User identifiers are **double-hashed** before transmission, making them impossible to trace back to you
- TelemetryDeck does not use cookies, fingerprinting, or cross-app tracking
- You can **opt out** at any time in Settings > Privacy > Anonymous Usage Analytics

## Contacts Access

- The app accesses your device contacts solely to display names for relationship tracking
- Contact data is read on-demand and never stored separately
- Only names and identifiers are used — no phone numbers or emails are stored in app data

## Local Storage

- All app data is stored locally using Apple's Core Data framework
- Data is encrypted at rest by iOS
- No cloud sync in the current version

## Notifications

- The app uses local notifications only
- No push notification servers are involved
- Notification content is generated entirely on-device
